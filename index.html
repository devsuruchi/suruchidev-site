Common Queries

What conditions do you treat at Axis Physiotherapy?

We specialize in sports injuries (ACL, Meniscus), back pain, neck stiffness, and post-operative rehabilitation for patients across Kathmandu and Lalitpur.

Do I need a doctor's referral for physiotherapy in Nepal?

No, you can book a direct consultation with our specialists at Axis Physiotherapy for an initial assessment without a prior referral.

What is Dry Needling and is it available in your clinic?

Yes, we offer Dry Needling in our Lalitpur terminal. It is a modern medical technique used to treat muscular pain and myofascial trigger points.
